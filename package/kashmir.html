Kashmir Packages

5 Nights , 4Days

Here’s a detailed itinerary for a 5-night, 4-day Kashmir package covering Gulmarg, Pahalgam, Sonamarg, Srinagar local sightseeing, and Dal Lake, including hotel stays with breakfast and dinner:

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar
• Arrive at Srinagar Airport.
• Transfer to your hotel in Srinagar.
• Check-in and freshen up.
• Rest of the day at leisure.
• Dinner at the hotel.

Day 2: Srinagar Local Sightseeing
• Breakfast at the hotel.
• Visit Mughal Gardens (Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh, Chashme Shahi).
• Explore the Shankaracharya Temple for panoramic views.
• Enjoy a Shikara ride on Dal Lake, visiting floating gardens and houseboats.
• Dinner at the hotel.

Day 3: Gulmarg Excursion
• Breakfast at the hotel.
• Drive to Gulmarg (Approx. 50 km, 1.5-2 hours).
• Enjoy the Gondola ride to Phase 1 and Phase 2 (at your own expense).
• Explore Gulmarg meadows and indulge in activities like horse riding or simply enjoy the scenic beauty.
• Return to Srinagar in the evening.
• Dinner at the hotel.

Day 4: Pahalgam and Sonamarg Excursion
• Breakfast at the hotel.
• Drive to Pahalgam (Approx. 90 km, 2.5-3 hours).
• Visit Betaab Valley, Aru Valley, and Chandanwari (if time permits).
• Enjoy activities like river rafting on the Lidder River (Optional).
• Proceed to Sonamarg (Approx. 80 km from Pahalgam, 2-2.5 hours).
• Explore Thajiwas Glacier and indulge in local sightseeing.
• Return to Srinagar in the evening.
• Dinner at the hotel.

Day 5: Departure from Srinagar
• Breakfast at the hotel.
• Check-out from the hotel.
• Depending on your flight/train schedule, you can visit local markets for shopping or explore any missed attractions.
• Transfer to Srinagar Airport/Railway Station for your onward journey.

Package Inclusions:
• Accommodation in a standard/deluxe hotel for 5 nights.
• Daily breakfast and dinner at the hotel.
• Transportation for all sightseeing excursions in a comfortable vehicle.
• Shikara ride on Dal Lake.
• All applicable taxes and service charges.

Package Exclusions:
• Airfare/Train fare.
• Gondola ride in Gulmarg.
• River rafting in Pahalgam.
• Personal expenses, tips, and any additional services not mentioned in the package.
• Lunch, snacks, and any meals not mentioned in inclusions.
